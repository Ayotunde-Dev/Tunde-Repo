How They Shape Your Life

Habits play a huge role in our daily lives, often shaping our success, health and happiness. Whether it is excersizing, reading, or waking up early, good habits can help us grow, while bad habits can hold us back. In this article, we'll explore how habits work, why they matter and how you can build positive ones that last.

What Are Habits?

A habit is a behaviour that we do automatically, often without thinking. Brushing your teeth, drinking tea in the morning are habiits formed by repetiton

The Habit Loop

According to research, habots follow a 3-step cycle:
  • Cue (Triggeer) - something that prompts the habit
  • Routine (Action) - The behaviour itself
  • Reward (Benefit) - The result that reinforces the habit

Why Are Habits Important?

  • They Save Mental Energy
  • They Shape Your Identity
  • They create Long-Term Results

How To Build Good Habits?

  • Start Small
  • Use Triggers
  • Track your progress

How To Break Bad Habits

  • Identify Triggers
  • Replace them, dont just remove
  • Make it harder

Conclusion

Habits shape our life in powerful ways. By understanding how they work and applying small changes, you can build habits that support your goals and improve your daily life.
