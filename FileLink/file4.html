Understanding Mental Health

Mental health is an essential aspect of our overall well-being, influencing how we feel think and act. It is as important as physical health yet it is often overlookeed or stigmatized. This article will explore what mental health is, why it is crucial and how you can maintain a healthy mind.

What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to the state of a person's psychological and scoial well being. It affects how individuals cope with stress, relate to others, and make decisions.

The Importance of Mental Health

Common Mental Health Challenges

Maintaining Mental Health

Maintaining mental health requires adequate excersize, eating the right type of meal and adequate rest.
