What Is a Research?

Research is the process of systematically investigating a topic to discover new information.

Types of Research

  • Basic Research
  • Applied Research
  • Qualitative Resarch
  • Quantitative Research

Research Writing

Research writing is a structured way of presenting findings from an investigation or study.

Steps Involved in Research Writing

  • Choose a Research Topic
  • Conduct Preliminary Research
  • Develop a Research Question or Thesis Statement
  • Gather and Evaluate Sources
  • Create an Outline
  • Write the Firtst Draft
  • Revise and Edit
  • Site Sources
  • Submit and Share
